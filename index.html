A Weekend with Damien

A brief overview of the wonder that is Damien's weekend

Friday

My Fridays usually start out with some class and then I hold a lecture for a new experimental class here at UMD, followed by office hours!   I teach a class about how to prepare for interviews and be successful in interviews.

Saturday

This past Saturday I met up with some friends and played Magic: the Gathering all day, it's a blast! We ended the day by going out for dinner in Bethesda.

Magic is a trading card game and I will briefly explain the rules. Usually each player brings his/her own deck of cards to a game. The game is played 1v1 but can also be played During a 2 player game each player takes a turn, the player that goes first is determined at random, and when playing best of 3, in the following games the player that lost the last game goes first.

Each player starts with 20 life and 7 cards in his/her hand.

  1. Upkeep
  2. Draw Step
  3. Main Phase
  4. Combat
  5. Main Phase
  6. End Step

Sunday

Sundays are usually when I unwind from all of the strenuous activities from the weekend. This is when I will work on my various art pieces and if the weather is nice, go outdoors and practice my photography. It's important for your mental health to have stress-relieving hobbies.
